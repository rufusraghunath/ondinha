Capoeira for beginners

Ondinha has been a student, practitioner, and teacher of capoeira since 2010. Based in Berlin, he has taught all over the world, including in the US, Germany, India, China, and Singapore. His classes are based on the foundational elements of conditioning, technique, and play. Ondinha also offers personal training, as well as workshops on floreios (soft acrobatics).
