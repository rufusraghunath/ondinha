Nossos princípios

"Capoeira é para homem, menino e mulher. Só não aprende quem não quer." — Mestre Pastinha

"Capoeira é para todos." — Mestre Bimba

We envision a world in which the incredible potential of capoeira is realized for all. Moving towards this future is our mission.

On this journey, we have come to value:

  • Empowering the practitioner
  • Adapting to context while honoring the spirit of capoeira
  • Strong communities based on openness, trust, and accountability
  • Seeking out and embracing complexity

These principles deeply inform our daily work as teachers. We invite you to ask questions and engage us in dialogue about them.

This manifesto was co-authored by Instrutor Ondinha and Professor Popô, based on decades of capoeira experience and friendship.
